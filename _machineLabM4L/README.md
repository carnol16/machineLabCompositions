# MachineLab4L

## first off, YOU'RE WELCOME

this is a compilation of max for live devices that allow you to compose for the machine lab from anywhere. they include sample libraries allowing you to access the sounds of the lab while also handling the OSC communication necessary to control the instruments of the lab locally. while connected to the Skynet network, click the top left button to switch between local sampling and OSC communication.

they integrate with ableton's midi systems (reference each instrument's midi range), along with a generative button allowing for on-the-fly generative composition. click the "gen" button to activate generative mode, press space bar to start the ableton transport, then click the instrument's representation in the UI to determine the probability of each note. "subd" determines the subdivision relative to BPM and "chance" determines overall probability.

if you want to edit devices, sometimes it will lose track of the audio files. to solve this, unfreeze the device, drag in the all the samples for that anywhere in the device, re-freeze the device and save.

feel free to click around, take them apart, make some cool sounds and have fun !!
